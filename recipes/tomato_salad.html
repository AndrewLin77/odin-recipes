Tomato Salad

Description

Tomato and mozzarella salad with fresh basil... There's nothing better than a caprese salad, especially in the summer if you can get tomatoes and basil from your garden.

Ingredients

  • 3 large tomatoes, sliced
  • 8 ounces fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • ¼ cup balsamic vinegar
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ¼ cup minced fresh basil

Directions

  1. Place tomato slices, alternating with mozzarella slices, on a large serving platter.
  2. Combine oil, balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper in a jar with a tight-fitting lid; shake well.
  3. Drizzle over tomatoes and mozzarella; sprinkle with basil.
  4. Enjoy!
